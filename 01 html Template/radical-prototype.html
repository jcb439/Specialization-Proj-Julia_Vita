RADICAL PROTOTYPE
Julia Beitel & Vita Sipayung
How might we…

...recognize genuine environmental care through everyday actions, not just words?

...reframe material practices—repairing, gardening, swapping, sharing—as civic participation equal to voting or protesting?

...surface contradictions between what people say and what they do, in ways that invite reflection rather than blame?

...build infrastructures where communities see themselves not as consumers, but as active participants shaping the future?
