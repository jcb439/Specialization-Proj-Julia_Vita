Project Questions

How do we recognize genuine care for the environment--beyond words--when participation shows up in actions, objects, and everyday practices?

- What does participation look like when it's done through actions, not just speech?

- What counts as "participation"?

- Do individuals actually care about the environment?

- How can radical software prototypes expose hidden ecological and social costs of everyday actions?
