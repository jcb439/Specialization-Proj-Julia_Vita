Alternative Trajectories & Outcomes

Every design decision creates divergent possibilities. Our forking paths diagram maps the various trajectories our intervention could take, exploring both optimistic and cautionary futures. Each path reveals potential outcomes based on different adoption patterns, user responses, and market conditions.

Understanding the Paths

This diagram illustrates the critical decision points and potential outcomes for our intervention. Each fork represents a moment where design choices, user behavior, or external factors could push the project in different directions—from widespread adoption and meaningful behavior change to user fatigue and abandonment.
